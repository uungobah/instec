Magnetic Testing

BM has certified experienced Level II MT technicians dedicated to providing accurate, cost-effective results to our customers. Our teams are experienced in field work in our nondestructive testing and materials. MT is an effective method for revealing surface and near surface cracks or defects in a wide variety of manufactured materials and components, including castings, forgings and weldments.

Magnetic Particle testing has many uses, both in the lab and in the field. MT is effective for final inspection, receiving inspection, in process inspection and quality control, maintenance and overhaul in the transportation industries, plant and machinery maintenance and the inspection of large components.

Benefit Include :

  • Can detect both surface and near subsurface indications
  • Can inspect large and irregular shape easily
  • Surface preparation not as critical
  • Indications are immediately visible directly on the specimen surface
